## Welcome!

I'm excited to launch NYTEMODE Academy — a free learning platform focused on AI, marketing, and emerging tech.

## Why I Built This

AI is transforming everything. But most educational content is either:
- Too basic and surface-level
- Too expensive for most people
- Outdated within months

I wanted to create something different: **practical, up-to-date education that anyone can access**.

## What to Expect

### Free Courses

All foundational courses are completely free. You'll learn:

- **Prompt Engineering** - Master the art of AI communication
- **AI Tools** - Hands-on guides to the best AI products
- **Creative AI** - Generate images, music, and more

### AI News

Stay updated with curated AI news and analysis. I'll break down:

- Major announcements from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.
- Practical implications for your work
- Tools and techniques worth your attention

### Premium Content

For those who want to go deeper, premium membership unlocks:

- Advanced courses
- Discord community access
- Direct support

## Get Started

The best way to start is with our **Prompt Engineering 101** course. It's free, practical, and will immediately improve how you work with AI.

Stay tuned for more content!
